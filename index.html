Bootstrap has its own reponsive paddings and margins Whein its classes, m stan for margin p stands padding
Bootstrap Padding and Margin can be applies on sides
  • t - set either margin or padding at the top of our element: mt or pt
  • b - set either margin or padding at the bottom side of our element: mb or pb
  • y - set either margin or padding on y-axis, on both top and bottom: my or py
  • x - sets either margin and padding on x-axis, both left and right sides: mx or px
